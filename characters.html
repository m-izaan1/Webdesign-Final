Characters

Meet the brave heroes of Expedition 33

Main Characters

Gustave

A devoted engineer and the de facto leader of Expedition 33. Wields both gun and sword, using his Overload ability for devastating damage.

Leader Engineer
Maelle

A 16-year-old fencer seeking freedom and self-discovery. Switches between Offensive, Defensive, and Virtuose stances in combat.

Fencer Stance Master
Renoir

A mysterious figure who guards the Paintress. Uses light-based spells and wields a sword of light in combat.

Guardian Light Mage
Lune

An Elemental Mage driven by a thirst for knowledge. Generates and consumes five types of Elemental Stains to enhance her skills.

Mage Elementalist

Team Dynamics

Character Synergies

Each character in Expedition 33 can combine their abilities with others to create powerful combo attacks. Experiment with different team compositions to discover devastating synergies that can turn the tide of battle.

Character Relationships

As you progress through the story, bonds between characters will grow. These relationships affect dialogue, unlock special story moments, and can even provide combat bonuses during critical battles.
