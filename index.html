The Story

The story follows a group of explorers traveling across a frozen world called Atlas. Every 33 years, a mysterious curse returns and brings great danger to humanity. The members of Expedition 33 set out to find the truth behind this curse and stop it forever. Along the way, they face harsh lands, strange creatures, and emotional choices that test their courage and hope.
